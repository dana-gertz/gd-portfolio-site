Hello, there!

My name is Dana Gertz, and I'm a graphic designer based in NYC. My work is conceptual and often nerd-flavored (not the candy), with a strange affinity for craft brews and obscure television references. I currently spend most of my time at SimpleReach, the innovative tech startup, ensuring that nothing except memes are typeset in Comic Sans and acting as an unofficial Tolkien consultant.

I do side projects occasionally, so feel free to contact me if you are in need of a designer, typographer, illustrator, photographer, ice cream taste tester, or someone to absolutely destroy in any existing N64 game.
